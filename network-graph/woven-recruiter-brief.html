The problem

You're hired. You have a brief.
And you're starting cold.

Your client knows exactly who they want. They've worked with these people. They've seen them present. They've hired them before. That knowledge lives in their LinkedIn — and right now it's invisible to you.

Cold outreach on a warm network is waste. You're not looking for strangers. You're looking for people your client already trusts — you just can't see them yet.

Woven closes the gap.

How it works

Three steps from brief to warm path.

01 — Upload

One file from your client.

Your client exports their LinkedIn data — one CSV, five minutes. You upload it to Woven. Their entire network is now a searchable graph.

02 — Map

Warmth, not just connections.

Woven builds a live network — nodes for every connection, edges weighted by warmth. Messages, recommendations, shared history. Who they actually know, not just who they're linked to.

03 — Hire

The shortest warm path.

Search by role or company. Woven shows you every candidate and who should make the introduction — ranked by relationship strength before you pick up the phone.

The warmth difference

Not all connections are equal.

A 1st-degree connection who exchanged three messages three years ago is not the same as someone who left a written recommendation. Woven knows the difference.

Every edge in the graph carries a warmth score — built from message frequency, recency, recommendations, and who reached out first.

Your intro strategy is ranked before you make the first call. You lead with the warmest path. Every time.

"The best candidates are already in your client's network. Woven just makes them findable."

The recruiter workflow

No guessing. No "let me check your LinkedIn." The network is already in front of you — organised, weighted, and searchable by the people who matter most.

Every client brief becomes a rich map you can navigate from day one. Not better networking. Richer hiring.

Copy guardrails

Enforced rules for all copy — headlines, body, CTAs, labels.

Never
  • The word "platform"
  • "AI-powered" — Woven is graph math, not LLM inference
  • Exclamation marks
  • Passive voice constructions
  • "Leverage" or "unlock"
  • Startup filler ("game-changing", "seamless")
Always
  • Short sentences. Active voice.
  • "Richer hiring" not "better networking"
  • Name the warmth concept — it's the product's core
  • Speak to the recruiter as a practitioner, not a buyer
  • Calm confidence — this tool works, it doesn't shout
  • Specificity over adjectives ("9.5 warmth score" not "deep connection")
